Description In response to the environmental impact of commercially produced beverages, our project "Quelle" introduces a sustainable and interactive alternative using local tap water. Quelle integrates a user-friendly app with an innovative vending machine, designed to provide customizable, eco-friendly drink options. The app allows users to personalize their drinks with various flavors and sugar alternatives, suitable for all, including diabetics.

The heart of Quelle is its vending machine, which is directly connected to the local water supply. This machine uniquely combines selected flavors and sweeteners with water to craft each drink on demand. Its design includes a transparent section, allowing users to witness their drinkâ€™s creation, thereby creating a connection between the customer and the production process.

Complementing our sustainable approach, Quelle features a reusable glass bottle with a cap that doubles as a cup, reducing plastic waste. Our development process involved thorough research, expert consultations, and multiple prototype iterations, including a life-sized model for user testing. This comprehensive effort resulted in a system that not only offers a fun and personalized beverage experience but also significantly cuts down on environmental impact, presenting a modern solution to traditional soft drink consumption.
