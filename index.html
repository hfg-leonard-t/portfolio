About me

I'm a dedicated tech enthusiast with a passion for web development and the creation of hands-on prototypes, especially using Arduino and other microcontrollers. I have interests that go beyond my work, as I enjoy bouldering, swimming, and engaging in hobbies like 3D printing, assembling PCs, and exploring AR-VR technologies. My expertise in web develop-ment is backed by solid experience in JavaScript, HTML, CSS, React.js, Vue.js, C++ and more. This background fuels my excite-ment for crafting and improving full stack experiences. Additionally, my hands-on app-roach shines in hardware prototype building,

where I excel in bringing ideas to life through functional prototypes and/or test setups. Through my studies I'm also adept in UI/UX design, ensuring that the interfaces I build are not only aesthetically pleasing but also user-friendly. Beyond my technical skills, I'm known for my punctuality, organization, and reli-ability. I thrive in team environments, com-fortably adapting to either collaborative or leadership roles. My skills and qualifications are broad and versatile, and I'm excited to apply them in my next professional chapter.
