Semester Practical Semester (Winter Semester 2022)
Design agency Futurice.com
Tasks Frontend Development & UI/UX Design
Description During my practical semester, I contributed significantly to the web development for the Red Bull Munich Ice Hockey team's website (redbullmuenchen.de). My primary responsibilities included redesigning and reprogramming the header carousel, enhancing the website's responsiveness and usability, and developing a versatile menu that works nicely for both mobile and desktop users. These components were also adapted for use on the website of SAP Garden, the team's new ice hockey arena (sapgarden.com). The components I enhanced and created are live today on both websites. In addition to my web development tasks, I also gained valuable experience in UX/UI Design, focusing on user experience and interface design.
